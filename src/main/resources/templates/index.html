Want to rule your very own kingdom?
Of course you don't. That sounds like a lot of work. That's why you should choose someone else to do it.

But first, you do need to put a tiny fraction of effort into creating your very own dictatorship. How else will you know how much people hate you and your ideas? Simply click on "Create a Party" to get started. Make sure you put an appropriate amount of thought into your responses, as poor creation attempts will be mocked mercilessly on Twitter.

Like a real dictatorship, others are free to support or rise against you as they see fit, with little to no consequences for their actions. You can try to create a dictatorship that has the highest number of loyal followers, or one that everyone wants to see burn to the ground. Whatever you want to do is fine; we're not here to judge you to your face.
